# Clear Ask Matrix: P1 Sections
## NDAA FY2026 (S.2296)

**Date:** January 2026  
**Purpose:** Structured asks for program office engagement

## MATRIX

| Execution Path | Target Office Type | Authority Lever | The Ask | Proof Required |
|----------------|-------------------|-----------------|---------|----------------|
| **Defense Facility Infrastructure** | Service Construction Offices (NAVFAC, AFCEC, IMCOM) | Procurement | Include wireless power in authorized MILCON project specifications during design phase. | Cost-benefit analysis showing battery elimination savings, integration feasibility study |

## AUTHORITY LEVER DEFINITIONS

**Funding:** Direct authorization of appropriations or funding mechanisms  
**Procurement:** Authority to acquire goods, services, or technology  
**Mandate:** Requirement or directive to take specific action  
**Pilot:** Authority to conduct demonstration or pilot programs

**Operational Documentation:**
