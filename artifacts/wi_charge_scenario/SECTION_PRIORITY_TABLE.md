## AUTHORITY TYPE DEFINITIONS

### Funding
- **Definition:** Direct authorization of appropriations or funding mechanisms
- **Action Required:** Identify funding amounts, application processes, eligibility requirements
- **Engagement:** Appropriations committees, program offices with budget authority

### Procurement
- **Definition:** Authority to acquire goods, services, or technology
- **Action Required:** Identify procurement pathways, contracting mechanisms, requirements
- **Engagement:** Acquisition offices, contracting officers, program managers

### Mandate
- **Definition:** Requirement or directive to take specific action
- **Action Required:** Identify compliance requirements, implementation timelines, reporting
- **Engagement:** Implementing offices, compliance officers, program managers

### Pilot
- **Definition:** Authority to conduct demonstration or pilot programs
- **Action Required:** Develop pilot proposals, identify pilot sites, establish success metrics
- **Engagement:** Program offices, pilot program managers, evaluation offices

### Reporting
- **Definition:** Requirement to report on specific topics or activities
- **Action Required:** Identify reporting requirements, data needs, submission deadlines
- **Engagement:** Reporting offices, congressional committees, oversight bodies

## ENGAGEMENT TYPE DEFINITIONS

### MILCON Alignment
- **Definition:** Integration with Military Construction (MILCON) projects
- **Stakeholders:** Service construction offices (NAVFAC, AFCEC, IMCOM), MILCON program managers
- **Timeline:** Aligns with construction project timelines (12-24 months)

### ERCIP Application
- **Definition:** Energy Resilience and Conservation Investment Program funding application
- **Stakeholders:** ERCIP Program Office, DLA Energy, Service Energy Offices
- **Timeline:** Annual application cycles, 6-12 month approval process

### Energy Savings Reinvestment
- **Definition:** Using energy cost savings to fund additional efficiency projects
- **Stakeholders:** Service Energy Offices, Installation Energy Managers, DLA Energy
- **Timeline:** Ongoing, as savings accrue

### SBIR/STTR
- **Definition:** Small Business Innovation Research / Small Business Technology Transfer programs
- **Stakeholders:** Service SBIR/STTR program offices, topic authors, program managers
- **Timeline:** Phase I (6 months), Phase II (24 months), Phase III (ongoing)

### DIU CSO
- **Definition:** Defense Innovation Unit Commercial Solutions Opening
- **Stakeholders:** DIU, Service innovation offices, acquisition offices
- **Timeline:** 90-day proposal-to-award cycle

### Priority Energy Projects
- **Definition:** Expedited consideration for high-impact energy projects
- **Stakeholders:** ASD EI&E, Service Energy Offices, DLA Energy
- **Timeline:** Expedited approval (30-60 days)

### International Coordination
- **Definition:** Coordination with international partners on technology adoption
- **Stakeholders:** USD R&E, Defense Technology Security Administration, International Offices
- **Timeline:** Ongoing coordination, 6-12 month cycles

### R&D Center Enhancement
- **Definition:** Enhancement of defense R&D center capabilities
- **Stakeholders:** Defense R&D Centers, USD R&E, R&D Program Offices
- **Timeline:** Annual funding cycles, 12-24 month projects

### Technology Enhancement
- **Definition:** Technology enhancement programs for specific applications
- **Stakeholders:** Service R&D offices, Program Offices, Technology Managers
- **Timeline:** Project-specific, 6-18 months

### Innovation Hubs
- **Definition:** Public-private innovation hub partnerships
- **Stakeholders:** Innovation Hubs, DIU, Advanced Manufacturing Programs
- **Timeline:** Ongoing partnerships, 12-24 month cycles

### DOE NNSA
- **Definition:** Department of Energy National Nuclear Security Administration programs
- **Stakeholders:** DOE NNSA, Energy Infrastructure Offices, Facility Managers
- **Timeline:** Annual funding cycles, 12-24 month projects
